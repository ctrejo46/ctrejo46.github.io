Aspiring Artist

Hello! My name is Chris and I am an aspiring artist who thrives on pushing my ability to the limits in order to achive a great result. I'm very much a beginner but love to see the progress in my drawing and coloring technique with each picture.

Photoshop

Working with Photoshop from my previous application "Krita" has really helped me in my ability to draw, sketch and color. I've been using photoshop for at least a couple months. I had originally starting using Photoshop back in the 10th 11th and 12th grade of Highschool, although I had a lot of trouble tryring to get used to the program. Things have since shot off since I've purchased photoshop for myself, and here I am today.

Blender

I knew Blender was around for things such as high end production movies, but I never really thought about trying it out. I did end up trying it around 2018, this was before Blender version 2.8 was released, but had an extremely hard time learning it. I waited a couple of years and then gave it another shot, it is now 2020 and although I have still very much to learn, I'm having a ton of fun using the program.
